    Notes on Construction

    • SSI: weighted count of delayed/suppressed releases (NSS, CES, PLFS, Census, ASI), revisions, and methodology changes (weights: timeliness 0.4, completeness 0.3, revision frequency 0.3). Scaled 0‚Äì10.
    • FCI: cesses+surcharges as % of gross taxes, conditional transfers share, GST Council decisions favouring centre (principal component).
    • DQI: composite of civil liberties, media plurality, electoral competition metrics (rescaled to 0‚Äì1).

    Index Construction (Sketch)

    SSI (0‚Äì10)

    SSI_t = 10 * (0.4*T_t + 0.3*C_t + 0.3*R_t)

    T: timeliness (0‚Äì1), C: completeness (0‚Äì1), R: revisions (0‚Äì1).

    FCI (0‚Äì1)

    FCI_t = PC1(cess%, cond.transfer%, GST tilt)

    Principal component scaled to [0,1].

    DQI (0‚Äì1)

    DQI_t = rescale(w1*CL + w2*MP + w3*EC)

    Civil liberties, media plurality, electoral competition.
