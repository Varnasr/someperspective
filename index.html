Novel Indices Developed

Statistical Suppression Index (SSI)

Measures delays and cancellations of government surveys. Weighted by survey importance and historical frequency.

Fiscal Centralization Index (FCI)

Tracks concentration of revenue and spending power. Combines tax devolution, grants, and borrowing limits.

Democratic Quality Index (DQI)

Composite of judicial independence, media freedom, civil liberties, and electoral integrity measures.

Employment Quality Index (EQI)

Combines formalization, wage levels, job security, and social protection coverage metrics.

Data Triangulation

Given systematic data suppression, we employ multiple validation techniques:

  1. Cross-validation with international databases
  2. Proxy indicators from private sector sources
  3. State-level data aggregation where national data is missing
  4. Satellite data for economic activity measurement
  5. Corporate disclosures and regulatory filings
